Welcome to Café Noir

Nestled in the heart of the city, Café Noir has been a beloved gathering place since 1978, offering rich, aromatic coffee and an inviting atmosphere for coffee lovers and creatives alike. Founded by a passionate roaster with a vision to bring European-style coffee culture to the neighborhood, Café Noir quickly became known for its signature dark-roasted blends, hand-crafted pastries, and warm hospitality.
